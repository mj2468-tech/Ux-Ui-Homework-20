Start From Scraps Case Study

This is Start From Scraps: a UxUi Case Study that focuses on saving money and preventing food waste by giving customers recipe ideas based on what they already have in their pantires/refrigerator.

It uses utility classes for typography and spacing to space content out within the larger container.

Problem Statement

Inefficient food usage, exacerbated by a lack of insight into consumer behavior with random fridge items, contributes to significant wastage. Addressing this issue requires understanding these behaviors. As we develop an app to assist users in optimizing food usage, \ it's vital to investigate consumer practices, quantify wastage, and devise strategies for efficient usage.
